LIC - (Life Insurance Corporation of India)

Life Insurance Corporation of India (LIC) is an Indian state-owned insurance group and investment company headquartered in Mumbai. It is the largest insurance company in India with an estimated asset value of over Rs. 31 trillion (US$ 419 billion). LIC offers a wide range of life insurance products to cater to the various financial needs of its customers.
